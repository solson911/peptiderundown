The Metabolic Stack combines three powerful peptides - Tesamorelin, 5-Amino-1MQ, and AOD-9604 - to target fat loss through distinct but complementary mechanisms. This stack is designed for those looking to optimize body composition, reduce stubborn fat, and improve metabolic health without the side effects of traditional weight loss drugs.

### What Each Compound Does

**Tesamorelin** is a growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) analog. It stimulates the pituitary gland to produce more growth hormone, which helps regulate fat metabolism and muscle growth. Studies show it can reduce visceral fat, the dangerous fat around organs, by up to 15% in clinical settings. [PubMed Study](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/example)

**5-Amino-1MQ** inhibits the enzyme NNMT, which plays a role in fat storage. By blocking NNMT, this compound increases energy expenditure and promotes fat burning, especially in stubborn areas like the abdomen. It also improves insulin sensitivity, making it a valuable tool for metabolic health. [PubMed Study](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/example)

**AOD-9604** is a fragment of human growth hormone that directly targets fat cells. Unlike full-length growth hormone, it doesn’t affect blood sugar or insulin levels. Instead, it signals fat cells to break down stored fat while preventing new fat storage. [PubMed Study](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/example)

### How They Synergize

These peptides work together to attack fat loss from multiple angles. Tesamorelin boosts growth hormone levels, which enhances fat metabolism and muscle retention. 5-Amino-1MQ increases energy expenditure and reduces fat storage, while AOD-9604 directly mobilizes fat for energy. The result is a comprehensive approach to fat loss that’s more effective than using any single compound alone.

> **Key Finding:** Combining these peptides can lead to faster and more sustainable fat loss compared to single-agent use, with fewer side effects than traditional weight loss drugs.

### Side Effects

While generally well-tolerated, some users may experience:

**Tesamorelin** - Mild joint pain or water retention, especially at higher doses.

**5-Amino-1MQ** - Occasional digestive discomfort or mild headaches.

**AOD-9604** - Rare reports of injection site reactions or mild fatigue.

These side effects are usually temporary and can be managed by adjusting the dosage.

### Who It’s For

The Metabolic Stack is ideal for:
