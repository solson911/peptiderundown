## Domestic Travel with Peptides: TSA Guidelines

You can legally fly with peptides domestically when following TSA medication rules:

**Liquid peptide rules:**
- Containers ≤3.4 oz (100ml) fit in quart-sized bag
- Larger medically necessary quantities allowed with declaration
- Tell agents "These are injectable medications"

**Documentation essentials:**
- Doctor's prescription/note (even if not legally required)
- Original peptide packaging with labels
- Cold chain validation documents if applicable

**Pro tip:**
> "Keep peptides in original packaging with pharmacy labels. TSA sees hundreds of insulin kits daily - present yours similarly." - Former TSA Officer Interview, 2025

## Hotel Storage Solutions

**When refrigeration isn't available:**
1. Request medical fridge (show prescription)
2. Use bathroom sink cold water bath
3. Portable 12V car cooler (dual-purpose)
4. Insulated bag with frozen water bottle

> "I've kept peptides stable for 5 days using clay pot cooling - wrap vials in damp cloth inside terracotta." - Backpacker's Journal, 2025

## FAQ: Traveling with Peptides

**Can airport scanners damage peptides?**
X-ray machines won't affect peptide integrity. Millimeter wave scanners are also safe. Metal detectors don't emit ionizing radiation.

**How to handle time zone changes?**
- Adjust injection schedule gradually (1hr/day)
- Use 24hr format alarms
- Consult prescribing physician pre-travel

**What if customs confiscates peptides?**
1. Remain calm and cooperative
2. Present all documentation
3. Request written confiscation notice
4. Contact embassy if improperly seized

**Best travel-friendly peptides?**
1. Lyophilized TB-500 (stable, multi-dose)
2. Nasal spray peptides (no refrigeration)
3. Transdermal formulations
4. Pre-filled syringes with temp-stable carriers

**Emergency protocols:**
- Lost peptides: Contact local compounding pharmacies
- Temperature excursion: Stability testing strips
- Needle disposal: Use FDA-approved travel sharps containers

**Cruise ship considerations:**
- Notify medical staff upon boarding
- Verify fridge temperatures daily
- Bring 25% extra supply for itinerary changes
