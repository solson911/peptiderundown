## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long until I see results?

- **Skin:** 4-8 weeks for initial changes, 12+ weeks for full effects
- **Hair:** 3-6 months for noticeable regrowth

### Are peptides safe long-term?

Most show excellent safety profiles in studies lasting 6-12 months. Cycling (8-12 weeks on, 4 weeks off) is recommended for injectables.

### Can I use peptides with retinoids?

Yes. Peptides like Matrixyl work synergistically with retinol. Apply peptides first, wait 10 minutes, then apply retinoids.

### Do peptides replace botox?

No. Peptides improve skin quality but don't paralyze muscles. Many use both for thorough anti-aging.

### What's the best carrier for topical peptides?

Serums with pentylene glycol or dimethyl isosorbide enhance penetration. Avoid heavy creams that limit absorption.

### Can peptides cause hair shedding?

Rarely. BPC-157 may cause temporary shedding as weaker hairs are replaced by stronger ones ("dread shed" phenomenon).

### Are there blood tests to monitor effects?

- **Skin:** Procollagen type 1 C-terminal peptide (P1NP)
- **Hair:** Ferritin, vitamin D, TSH to rule out other causes
